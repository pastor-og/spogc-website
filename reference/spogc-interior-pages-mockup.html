I'm New · Start Here

Your first Sunday, sorted.

Whether it's your first time in any church or your first time back in years — here's everything you'd want to know before you come. Spoiler: you can wear flip-flops.

What to expect

The basics, no surprises

🌊

When & where

Sundays at 9:00 AM — Beach Church at the Boardwalk Pavilion in Ocean Grove. Off-season, we gather at 80 Embury Avenue.

🩴

What to wear

Whatever you'd wear to the beach or to brunch. Sandy feet are a feature, not a problem.

⏱️

How long

About an hour — music, a message that connects to real life, and zero pressure to do anything but be there.

🧒

Kids

Kids are fully welcome in worship, and our Anchored ministries have safe, fun spaces for every age.

Honest answers

The questions people actually ask

I haven't been to church in years. Will that be weird?

Not even a little. Nobody's keeping score, and nobody will put you on the spot. A lot of us came back after a long time away — that's kind of our thing.

Do I have to give money or sign anything?

No. The offering is for our church family — guests are exactly that: guests. Come, breathe the sea air, and let it be a gift to you.

I have doubts and questions about faith. Is that okay?

It's more than okay — questions are how faith grows. Bring them. And when you're ready, our Next Steps page has paths built for exactly where you are.

Where do I park?

Street parking fills early on summer Sundays — arrive by 8:40 for the easiest spots, or better yet, bike or walk the boardwalk like half the congregation does.
