I'm New · Start Here

Your first Sunday, sorted.

Whether it's your first time in any church or your first time back in years — here's everything you'd want to know before you come. Spoiler: you can wear flip-flops.

What to expect

Sunday in four facts

No surprises. Here's exactly what you're walking into.

🕘

When & where

Sundays at 9:00 AM — Beach Church at the Boardwalk Pavilion in Ocean Grove. Off-season, we gather at 80 Embury Avenue.

👕

Come as you are

Flip-flops, shorts, sand on your feet — we're a beach church. Dress for comfort, not a dress code.

🎵

What worship's like

A blend of hymns and modern songs, a message rooted in Scripture, communion open to everyone. About an hour, start to finish.

👶

Kids & families

Kids are welcome in worship — always. We love the sound of children in church. Quiet activities and coloring pages available.

Honest answers

Questions you'd actually ask a friend

Do I have to believe anything to come?

No. Come curious, come skeptical, come searching. You're welcome in the room while you're figuring it out.

What if I haven't been to church in years?

Then you're in good company — a lot of us started (or re-started) right where you are. Nobody's keeping score.

Is there coffee?

Yes. And it's good. Fellowship time before and after every service.

Where do I park?

Street parking fills early on summer Sundays — arrive by 8:40 for the easiest spots, or better yet, bike or walk the boardwalk like half the congregation does.

Tell us you're coming. We'll look for you.

Drop your name and we'll send one short text or email before Sunday — where to park, what's happening, and who to look for when you arrive. That's it.
